Philosophy
Philosophy

We at Chaitanya Vidyalaya, believe in building values beyond just education. It is this principle that is the driving force of our 30-year-old legacy. The environment at the school is created in nurturing the creative abilities of the students in an open and friendly atmosphere without undue pressure on the developing minds.
